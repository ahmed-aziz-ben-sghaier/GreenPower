OUR SERVICES

Explore unlimited possibilities

At GreenPower, we focus on leveraging Tunisia's abundant natural resources to propel the country toward a sustainable future. We believe in the transformative potential of renewable energy to not only reduce dependency on fossil fuels but also foster economic growth and environmental conservation. Our work is deeply rooted in the belief that local communities, industries, and governments must collaborate to build a future powered by clean, renewable energy.

Solar Photovoltaic Installation

We specialize in the design and installation of solar photovoltaic (PV) systems for both residential and commercial sectors. Our solutions help you generate clean, renewable energy while reducing your energy bills. Whether you're looking to power your home, business, or industrial operation, we provide customized solar installations that maximize efficiency.

Solar Power System Maintenance

To ensure long-term performance, we offer comprehensive maintenance services for solar power systems. This includes regular inspections, cleaning, and performance optimization to ensure your solar panels operate at peak efficiency throughout their lifespan.

Solar Panel Manufacturing Support

GreenPower is committed to fostering local manufacturing capabilities for solar panels and components. We collaborate with local manufacturers to provide high-quality solar equipment and create job opportunities in the renewable energy sector, contributing to Tunisia's green economy.

Energy Storage Solutions

We offer state-of-the-art solar energy storage solutions to help you store excess energy generated during the day for use at night or during peak demand periods. Our battery storage systems help enhance the reliability and self-sufficiency of your solar energy system.

Education and Training Programs

We believe that education is key to the future of renewable energy. GreenPower offers specialized training programs for engineers, technicians, and advocates of solar energy. These programs equip individuals with the knowledge and skills needed to maintain and optimize solar installations, driving Tunisia towards a sustainable energy future.

Solar Power Consulting

Our experts provide consulting services to guide businesses, municipalities, and individuals through the process of adopting solar energy. From assessing your energy needs to securing financing and navigating regulatory frameworks, we ensure a seamless transition to renewable energy.

Tozeur Solar Project

>The Tozeur Solar Project is a significant solar power initiative located in the southern part of Tunisia. This project contributes to the national energy grid by tapping into Tunisia's immense solar potential. It serves as a model for the scalability of solar photovoltaic systems across the country, especially in regions with high sunlight exposure, such as the southern governorates.
